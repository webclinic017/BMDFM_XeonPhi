##                                                    ##
########################################################

  0. Read latest information on our http://www.bmdfm.com official web site.


  1. Email the following information to bmdfm@bmdfm.de, your email should
     have subject "BMDFM license request":

                 My name: __________________________________________________
         My organization: __________________________________________________
                My email: __________________________________________________
              My address: __________________________________________________
         My INet-address: __________________________________________________
           My IP-address: __________________________________________________
           My HW-address: __________________________________________________
       My UNIX system ID: __________________________________________________

     For example:

           Email subject: BMDFM license request

                 My name: David Scott Freeman
         My organization: Flight Navigator Research Inc.
                My email: David.S.Freeman@flight-navigator-research.com
              My address: 615 Idlewyld Drive, Fort Lauderdale, FL 33301, USA
         My INet-address: big-iron.mainframes.flight-navigator-research.com
           My IP-address: 140.150.160.170
           My HW-address: 08:90:FE:A2:3B:55
       My UNIX system ID: "AIX IBMp-RS6000 1 7 00DF8EB46F00"
                          |this is the output of `uname -a`|

  2. Receive your license key (sequence of digits) via email and insert
     this license key to your BMDFMreg.inf file (you can also add/change the
     corresponding "LICENSE_FILE = BMDFMreg.inf" record in your BMDFMsrv.cfg
     file).


  3. How do you check the license:

       Start the BM_DFM Server. Type "STAT ON" at your BM_DFM Server console.
       If your license is invalid a warning message will appear.
